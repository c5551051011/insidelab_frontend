Large-Scale Vision-Language Models for Autonomous Driving: A Comprehensive Study

John Smith, Sarah Kim, Michael Johnson, Andrew Ng
📈 342 citations
⭐ 1.2K GitHub stars
💬 Oral presentation
📊 15 citations this year
This paper presents a comprehensive study of large-scale vision-language models for autonomous driving applications. We introduce a novel architecture that combines transformer-based vision encoders with language models to achieve state-of-the-art performance on multiple driving benchmarks. Our approach demonstrates significant improvements in scene understanding, object detection, and decision-making capabilities across diverse weather and lighting conditions.

Meta-Learning for Few-Shot Domain Adaptation in Neural Machine Translation

Lisa Chen, David Wilson, Andrew Ng
📈 156 citations
⭐ 892 GitHub stars
💬 Spotlight
We propose a meta-learning framework for few-shot domain adaptation in neural machine translation. Our method learns to quickly adapt translation models to new domains with minimal training data, achieving significant improvements over existing approaches. The framework is evaluated on multiple language pairs and domains, demonstrating its effectiveness and generalizability.
